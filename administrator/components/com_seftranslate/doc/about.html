About

Welcome to SEF Translate

Thank you for using our component!

SEF Translate is component for automatic translation of Joomla sites to languages that Google Translate and Bing/Microsoft Translator supports.

To see more details about SEF Translate and documentation, please visit page of SEF Translate.
If you have problems with SEF Translate, please visit our support forum.
We are also available for custom development. Please send your exact requirements at sales@ordasoft.com and we will get back to you with a quote. When you order a paid Customization, you will receive the next PRO version with your custom feature added.
